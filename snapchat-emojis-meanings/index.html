Birthday Setup in Snapchat

If you want your snap chatter to know your birthday, all you have to do is add your birthday in settings. Adding this feature will make your friends see the zodiac sign and the birthday cake emoji next to your username.

How to set your birthday

• Tap on settings emoji on your Snapchat profile screen

• Tap on My Account and then tap on Birthday

• Enter your birthday; be careful as you are allowed to change your birthday only for a limited number of times.
